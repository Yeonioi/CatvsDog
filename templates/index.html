✨ Best Conditions for Accurate Predictions

  • Clear & Well-Lit Photos: Use natural or good artificial lighting to ensure the subject is clearly visible
  • Actual Pets: The model works best with clear images of real cats and dogs
  • High Quality Images: Use JPG or PNG formats. Avoid heavily compressed or pixelated images
  • Good View: Front-facing or clear side views of the animal work best
  • Avoid Blur: Use sharp, focused images. Blurry photos reduce accuracy
  • Single Subject: Images with one clear cat or dog perform better than crowded scenes

Note: The model sometimes struggles and may force a prediction even on ambiguous images.
